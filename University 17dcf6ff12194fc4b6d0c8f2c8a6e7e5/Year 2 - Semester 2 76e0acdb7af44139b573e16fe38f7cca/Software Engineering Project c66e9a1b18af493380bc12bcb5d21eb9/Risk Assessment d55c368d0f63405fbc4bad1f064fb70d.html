Live Risks

No.RiskProbabilityImpactMitigation strategiesTriggers and Actions
1Data storage online gets destroyed, deleted or lost15Store data across a variety of different online platforms Create copies of data often and backup this data in secure places that cannot be affected by the internetTriggers The online services we are using release some sort of statement indicating that they may be outage for a period of time Actions Move stored online resources to another provider or make sure that the most recent version is up to date on the stored copy of the backup
2Task completion gets delayed either from team member illness, pressure from other deadlines or other unexpected events41Make sure every member of the team is always aware of the upcoming deadlines in which tasks need to be completed. If a member is unsure of whether they can complete all their assigned work by the deadline, inform the team early so that the task can be distributedTriggers Team member informs other teammates that they have contracted a cold/fever A big deadline is set for a specific team member in the future Actions Redistribute task so that the member with the illness or upcoming deadline can share some of the load of the task, making sure that they complete both tasks on time
3Project is not delivered on time to client23Use appropriate scrum and sprint planning techniques to make sure each deliverable is on time and the team does not fall behindTriggers The timings that have been calculated using the burn-down chart are not being met and the scheduled deliverables (especially the later ones) are not being finished by the specified dates Actions Cut back on ‘could’ requirements and focus solely on the ‘must’ and ‘should’ requirements in order to limit the team’s workload and make sure that the work is done in the specified time
4Information leak to other teams13Make sure any online platforms that are being used to cooperatively work on tasks are not shared with other people outside the teamTriggers Access to the team’s work is shared with another team or individual not in the team Actions Immediately stop working on the compromised work, make a copy and continue from left-off point on the new copy without allowing the other team/individual to see this work
5Finished deliverables and project do not meet client requirements22Repeatedly check in with the client whenever progress is made on the project to ensure that it is heading in a direction that the client is satisfied withTriggers Client informs that the most recent deliverable was not what they had envisioned or strayed slightly from the given requirements Actions Gather a better understanding of the specified requirements from the client through a professional meeting and then re-work the previous deliverable to match the requirements better
