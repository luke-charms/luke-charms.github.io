Circle Voting and Accumulator Space

All the centre points on a these circles will form another circle!

—> At the intersection, the point will form a circle that creates all the other circles

—> basically, use circles to find other circles… circle-ception

Pseudocode

accum=0
  for all x,y                                 !look at all points
     if edge(y,x)>threshold                   !check significance
        for r = min_r, max_r                  !do values of radius
					for theta = 0, 2*pi                 !go around a circle
						x0=x+r*cos(theta)                 !generate x
						y0=y+r*sin(theta)                 !generate y
						accum(y0, x0, r ) PLUS 1          !vote in accumulator
y0, x0 ,r = argmax(accum)                     !peak gives parameters

Arbitrary Shapes

Finding arbitrary shapes is as simple as:

  1. Use Generalised Hough transform
  1. Form (discrete) look-up-table (R-table)
  1. Vote via look-up-table

R-Table Construction

xc=xi−r∗cos(α)x_c=x_i-r*cos(\alpha)xc​=xi​−r∗cos(α)﻿

yc=yi−r∗sin(α)y_c=y_i-r*sin(\alpha)yc​=yi​−r∗sin(α)﻿

Procedure for GHT

Preparation

  1. Determine centre of template shape
  1. Form R-table from template shape

Application

  1. Use R-table to vote for points in the real image
    For edge points > threshold
Get edge direction(x,y)
For all R-table entries with direction(x,y)
	Vote in accumulator (@distance, @direction)
  1. Argmax(accumulator) gives centre co-ordinates of shape

Improvements that can be done
